Skript KD

(he/him/his)

Skript KD (given name, Michael Kunz) is a New York transplant by way of New Orleans. His chosen name is a play on "script kiddy", a derisive term used in the early days of the internet to describe (mostly) young males who would "hack" websites after learning simple JavaScript. Script kiddies were considered callow and annoying. They thought they were much more clever than they actually were. Despite supporting himself as a software engineer for the last several years, Michael still sees himself as a script kiddy. The two galleries on display here, Artifacts and Wildlife, are typical of Skript's art. Low resolution images enhanced digitally.
